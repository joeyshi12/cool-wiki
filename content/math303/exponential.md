The exponential random variable $X\sim Exp(\lambda)$, $\lambda > 0$
has a p.d.f. defined by $f(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x}$.

## Properties

- $\mathbb{E}[X] = 1/\lambda$
- $\min\{Exp(\lambda_1), Exp(\lambda_2)\} \sim Exp(\lambda_1 + \lambda_2)$
- $P(X > t + s \mid X > s) = P(X > t)$ (memoryless)
